# Impressum Plus and Impressum

For Impressum Plus we do not offer a separate trial version that can be installed and tried before buying. If you still want to test the plugin to try out the features or the compatibility with other plugins and themes, you can simply fall back on our free plugin [Impressum](https://wordpress.org/plugins/impressum/). Impressum cannot be used for all legal forms and does not have all the features of Impressum Plus, but should give you a good first impression.
